What's up- my name is Bryan Kurt Enriquez Tagay. I'm first-generation Filipino-American and many of us have long names. I'm also a gemini. I was born and raised in the Mission District of San Francisco where my vibrant and multicultural upbringing contributes to who I am today. My passions include art, fashion, and community. In my free time I like going to the gym, hanging out with friends, thrifting, eating good food, and exploring out to new places.

Besides going to SFSU for Visual Communication Design, I'm also currently I’m a part-time barista in the city's downtown area. One of my favorite parts of the job is making latte art. I’ve continued to carry this affinity for working with my hands allowing my creativity to manifest through tactile experiences. It's also been a great place to stay incredibly caffeinated and to meet new people everyday.

One of my favorite things to do is go to concerts, especially concerts that mosh and rock out. This concert was from Paris Texas' Mid Air show in Oakland.
